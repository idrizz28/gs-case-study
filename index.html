BJJ Academy — Texas
12:47 AM conversation · Booked in 9 minutes
Midnight Booking

A returning BJJ practitioner came in at 12:47 AM asking about class times and pricing. The bot handled the pricing deflection and booked him in 9 minutes. No staff involved.

12:47 AM LEAD: No I use to do BJJ years ago but the instructor moved.
               How much is the class and what are the times
12:48 AM BOT:   Oh awesome, welcome back to the mats! For class times, we have
               Adult Gi at 6:30 PM on Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday,
               plus Saturday at 10:00 AM. The first class is free so you can
               jump right in before we talk membership.
12:49 AM LEAD: Yes just for myself
12:49 AM BOT:   Perfect! Let's get you scheduled for a free trial class.
12:52 AM LEAD: [email provided]
12:54 AM BOT:   I've got openings tomorrow Friday at 6:30 PM, Tuesday the 26th
               at 6:30 PM, or Wednesday the 27th at 6:30 PM. What works best?
12:55 AM LEAD: Wednesday the 27th and then go from there
12:56 AM BOT:   ✓ APPOINTMENT BOOKED — Free Trial Class
               You're all set for Wednesday, May 27th at 6:30 PM.
Result: Booked at 12:56 AM. Zero staff involvement. Lead asked about pricing — bot redirected, collected all data, and closed in 9 minutes.

               His friend Jax attends there and he is interested in joining.

               to confirm, you're looking to get your son signed up for a trial class?
02:07 AM LEAD: Yes I think that would be great.
02:31 AM LEAD: 06/19/2016

02:33 AM BOT:   Perfect. He's 9, so he'll be in our Kids 8–10 Brazilian

02:51 AM BOT:   ✓ APPOINTMENT BOOKED — Free Trial Class

Result: Bot correctly routed to youth path, computed Kids 8–10 from DOB, captured child and parent info, and booked to the correct kids calendar. Parent confirmed with 👍.

Edge Case Coverage

Built for the hard cases.

Every scenario that would normally drop a lead or require staff intervention is handled in-flow.

👤
New adult lead
Full qualification → data capture → calendar booking, any time of day.
👦
Parent enrolling a child
Youth path with age-routing to the correct kids calendar. Parent info also captured.
👨‍👦
Parent + self enrolling
Multi-enrollee: both bookings completed in a single conversation session.
🚫
Under-18 self-booking
Minor gate fires → captures info → routes to team for guardian follow-up. No unsupervised booking.
💬
Pricing question
Firm redirect to trial class every time. No dollar figures given. Ever.
📋
Non-bookable program
Acknowledged without booking path. Redirected to trial for in-person context.
🤝
Staff takes over
One tag instantly disables the bot on any conversation. It never re-engages.
⚡
Hostile / opt-out
Graceful de-escalation. No booking attempt made. No argument.
